How to set this up in Vonage
  1. Go to Vonage Dashboard
  2. Click Numbers → Your Numbers
  3. Click on your number
  4. Under SMS, set Inbound Webhook URL to the URL above
  5. Click Save
  6. That's it — replies will appear in the Broadcast feed
