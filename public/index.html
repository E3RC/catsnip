About 10DLC

10DLC is a mandatory US carrier framework for A2P (application-to-person) SMS sent over long codes. Without it, T-Mobile and AT&T block or filter unregistered traffic. Verizon has been lenient but may begin enforcing soon. Registration costs a one-time fee (~$15 brand + ~$25 campaign) and takes 1–3 days for carrier approval.
